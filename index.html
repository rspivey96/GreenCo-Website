About Us

 GreenCo is a fully licensed, bonded, and insured company dedicated to recycling. From the beginning, we have always taken the initiative to recycle as much used material as possible, including the gutter aluminum and shingle materials.

 GreenCo is owned and operated by father and son, Rayburn Phillips & Rayburn Phillips II. From a humble beginning, both grew up working trade jobs in the construction business, learning the ins and outs of every part of a build. With more than 30 years of experience, GreenCo isn't motivated by big checks. We care more about taking care of you and your needs at the fairest price. We understand that every client's vision is different. With each project, our first priority is customer satisfaction. Our management and sales teams are grounded in this mission and we uphold the highest expectations for every project.
